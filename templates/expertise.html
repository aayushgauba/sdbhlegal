Our Area of Expertise

What We Do at SDBH Legal Associates

Family Law

We provide compassionate and effective legal advice on divorce, child custody, and alimony matters.

Matrimonial Litigations

Our firm deals with mutual consent divorce, contested divorce, maintenance, child custody, judicial separations, divorce cases of NRI's, counseling, etc. We understand the delicate and sensitive nature of matrimonial disputes, including issues like hurt feelings, disputes among couples, fidelity disputes, and financial adjustments.

Mutual Consent Divorce (Indian)

Mutual divorce is common between parties with free consent, understanding, and defusing matrimonial disputes including care and upbringing of children with mutual understanding.

Mutual Consent Divorce (NRI's)

Our firm serves NRI's for mutual divorce and other matrimonial disputes. We give special attention as we know that NRI's often find themselves in complex situations, sometimes affecting their overseas immigration status/career. NRI clients need not visit India for mutual divorce.

Contested Divorce

If parties have strong issues/reservations against each other, the interested party may approach our firm for a contested divorce or legal remedies.

Mediation

Mediation in India has become a viable alternative to traditional litigation, supported by a growing legal framework and increasing acceptance among disputants. Our expertise in mediation covers:

  • Legal Framework and Statutory Provisions
  • Court-Referred Mediation
  • Confidentiality in Mediation Proceedings
  • Mediator's Role and Qualifications
  • Mediation Agreements and Their Enforceability
  • Various Types of Disputes Suitable for Mediation
  • Institutional Support for Mediation
  • Cost-Effectiveness and Accessibility of Mediation
  • Pre-Litigation Mediation for Commercial Disputes

We are committed to promoting mediation as an efficient and amicable means of dispute resolution, offering our clients a pathway to resolve conflicts effectively and economically.
